About Docplanner Group

Docplanner Group is the leading digital healthcare platform in Europe, dedicated to making healthcare more accessible and human. Our mission is to bridge the gap between patients and healthcare providers through innovative technology.

Since our founding, we've grown to serve millions of patients and healthcare professionals across 13 countries. We believe that technology should empower both patients in their healthcare journey and doctors in their practice management.

Our Mission

To make the healthcare experience more human by connecting patients with the best healthcare providers and enabling them to manage their practice more effectively.
